  • What is From West Africa with Love?

    “From West Africa with Love” is an ecommerce website connecting West African textile sellers and craftsmen with people around the world who want to buy quality cloth and hire the services of West African craftsmen. The site combines the fun of dress up-games with online shopping to allow customers to select from different apparel designs and styles in creating one-of-a-kind custom fashion. For an eCommerce site to engage users in a fun shopping experience is widely unrecognized by small and large scale businesses.
